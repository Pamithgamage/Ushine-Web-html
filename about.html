Our Story

U Shine Batik was born from a love for Sri Lankan tradition and a desire to present it with a modern, elegant feel. Every saree is carefully designed, hand-crafted, and quality checked to ensure you receive a piece that feels special from the first touch.

From selecting fabrics to final finishing, we focus on detail, comfort, and durability—so you can wear confidence, not just clothing.
